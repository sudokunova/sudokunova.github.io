№ 05

Killer Sudoku, without mercy.

No starting numbers — just dotted regions with target sums. Each cage must hit its sum, with no repeats. Pure deduction from a blank canvas. Hard mode is where this variant shines.

№ 06

Math Match. Beat the clock.

Make the target by pairing cells. A brisk warm-up or a serious sprint — your call. The fastest of the variants, perfect for queue time.

№ 07

More ways to play.

The Explore tab keeps the menu of variants one tap away. Try a different mode on a coffee break. The same elegant board logic underneath, every time.

№ 08

Rotating challenges across every mode. Pro Series, Killer Instinct, Speed Match — a fresh lineup every Sunday. Stack up awards. Or play offline. Nothing forced.
